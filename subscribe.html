How to subscribe to Breaking Math

We're now on iTunes, and searchable on many podcasting apps! But if you don't find us for some reason, here are instructions on how to subscribe to Breaking Math. For instructions on how to install it on your device, first choose a player by clicking on one of the options below.

Choose a player:

If you have any difficulties subscribing to our podcast, don't hesitate to email us at breakingmathpodcast@gmail.com.

  • Podcasts (on iPhone)
  • Downcast
  • Most others (on Android, etc.)

Podcasts (on iPhone)

Here are some detailed instructions that should get Breaking Math installed on your device!

  1. Copy this link by holding down your finger on the link and then selecting copy: https://anchor.fm/s/7a32d64/podcast/rss
  2. Open Podcasts (you can do this by opening up your search bar by dragging down on the iPhone home screen. See the light arrow for details)
  3. Tap the + in the upper-left hand corner.
  4. Select Add Podcast
  5. Press and hold the box and hit Paste
  6. Tap Subscribe.
  7. Enjoy!

Downcast

Here are some detailed instructions that should get Breaking Math installed on your device!

  1. Copy this link by holding down your finger on the link and then selecting copy: https://anchor.fm/s/7a32d64/podcast/rss
  2. Open Downcast
  3. Tap Add on the bottom menu
  4. Tap Add Podcast Manually
  5. Paste it in the box under Feed or OPML Address (it should already be pasted)
  6. Tap Subscribe in the upper-right hand corner.
  7. Enjoy!

Most others

Here are some detailed instructions that should get Breaking Math installed on your device!

  1. Copy this link by holding down your finger on the link and then selecting copy: https://anchor.fm/s/7a32d64/podcast/rss
  2. Open your podcasting app.
  3. There should be something that says Add Podcast or Add Podcast Manually
  4. Paste the link there.
  5. Subscribe and enjoy!
